Why should we read secondhand book?

Whenever you are buying second hand textbooks, you are creating a path for recycling. In addition to recycling, you are able to save the lives of many trees. This is because more trees are destroyed for the paper manufacturing process. By using second hand books, you are able to reduce landfill waste.
